Project Idea: Automating the Clinical Coding System

UI Wireframes: iPad - iOS

User Sign In (using username and password)
This provides a familiar sign in experience that is similar to how hospital staff normally access other systems.

User Sign In (using 'Touch ID' fingerprint recognition)
This sign in option was considered as a faster alternative to username and password, however further research is required regarding acceptability of this approach considering relevant hospital data protection policies and procedures.

Post-login, Entering Patient Details
At this screen, the physician must enter the minimum patient data (full name, date of birth and one unique identifier) required for the app to search the patient database and return the patient details. The physician must then confirm these details are correct before proceeding.

Patient Confirmed, Entering Details for Coding
This is the data entry portion of the app as described in the report. Physicians are required to fill in all fields relating to the initial diagnosis and treatment, adding further diagnoses and treatments using the "plus" button at the top right of the screen.
